## 👤 About Me

<img align="right" width="340" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/74038190/229223263-cf2e4b07-2615-4f87-9c38-e37600f8381a.gif"/>

I'm a **Data Analyst & Statistician** based in **Dakar, Senegal 🇸🇳** with a Master's in **Statistics and Decision Science Informatics** (Biostatistics specialization).

With **2+ years of experience**, I help organizations extract value from their data — from raw numbers to clear, actionable insights that drive smarter decisions.

**🔬 Current Research**
Survival analysis of **gastric cancer in Senegal** — Kaplan-Meier estimators & Cox proportional hazard models on real clinical datasets.

**🎯 What I do best**
- Design end-to-end analytical pipelines
- Build interactive dashboards that tell compelling stories
- Apply statistical & ML models to solve real business problems
- Communicate findings to both technical and non-technical audiences
